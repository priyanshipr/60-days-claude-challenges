Day 3: Normal Prompt vs Role-Based Prompting

** Normal Prompt
A normal prompt is a direct instruction given to the AI without assigning any specific role or expertise.

Example

Prompt:
Explain Machine Learning.

Output
Machine Learning is a branch of Artificial Intelligence that allows
computers to learn from data and make predictions or decisions without
being explicitly programmed.

#Characteristics

.Generic response
.Limited context
.Basic explanation
.Suitable for quick answers
.May not match the target audience

** Role-Based Prompting

Role-Based Prompting means assigning a specific role, profession, or expertise to the AI before asking your question.
The AI then responds from the perspective of that role.

Example

Prompt:
You are an AI educator teaching complete beginners.
Explain Machine Learning in simple language.
Use real-world examples and avoid technical jargon.

Output
Imagine teaching a child how to identify different fruits.

The more examples you show, the better they become at recognizing
apples, bananas, and oranges.
Machine Learning works in a similar way. Instead of fruits, a computer
learns patterns from data and improves its ability to make predictions
over time.

#Characteristics

.More targeted response
.Better structure
.Audience-specific explanation
.Improved clarity
.More practical examples
